# your-next-lawyer — AI Legal Advisor v2.0 for Vietnamese law

> 3-layer citation verification, emergency triage, and a 14-persona legal council. AI assists — lawyers decide.

[![OpenClaw Skill](https://img.shields.io/badge/OpenClaw-Skill-blueviolet)](https://github.com/NachaFromMars)

## Overview
your-next-lawyer v2.0 is an AI legal advisor for Vietnamese law with strict quality controls. A 3-layer Citation Gate marks every claim ✅/⚠️/❌. Emergency Triage ("Red Screen") flags urgent cases. 14-persona council provides multi-angle analysis. Mandatory disclaimer on every output: AI does NOT replace lawyers.

## Features
- 3-layer Citation Gate (✅/⚠️/❌)
- Emergency Triage "Red Screen" + 5 diagnostic questions
- PII protection + redaction suggestions
- Quality Rubric — 5 criteria self-scoring
- 14 legal personas council
- 9-step workflow, 7 intake sets by legal area, 8 test cases
- Coverage: contracts, criminal, civil, labor, land, divorce, inheritance, complaints, legal drafting
